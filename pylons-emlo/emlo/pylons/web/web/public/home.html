"Lister reported that he had not seen an edition of Langius, but would like to peruse one. He mentioned that he was languishing of a cough and shortness of breath. He mentioned he had talked to Dr. Bason, and to the Bishop of Oxford on Lhwyd's behalf. Lister complained about his own work being satirized and the Newtonian physicians who base all their work on mathematics."

A letter to Frances Jessop from John Ray.
